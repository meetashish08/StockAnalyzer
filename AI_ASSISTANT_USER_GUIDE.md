## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Can I ask about stocks I don't own?**
A: Yes! The AI can analyze ANY stock, not just your holdings.

**Q: How real-time is the data?**
A: Stock prices are delayed by ~15 minutes (standard for free APIs).

**Q: Can the AI make buy/sell decisions for me?**
A: No, it provides analysis and recommendations. Final decision is yours.

**Q: What if I ask about an unsupported stock?**
A: The AI will tell you it's not in the available universe and suggest alternatives.

**Q: Can I trust the recommendations?**
A: Use them as one input for your decision. Always do your own research.
